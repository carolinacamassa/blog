A curated list of data learning resources for the quarantine

    resources

It’s March 14th 2020, and this week my country has come to a stop to because of the Covid-19 virus outbreak. All italians can expect to be quarantined in their homes for at least 20 more days.

We can also suppose that Italy won’t be the only nation on lockdown for long, which is why I’m writing in English.

Being stuck in your own home for weeks can be a harrowing experience, even more so when we are surrounded by such scary news and predictions.

Various people from the Data&AI community have been doing their part by sharing dashboards and statistical insights on the spread of the virus. Many businesses have gifted their digital services to italian citizens through the Solidarietà Digitale project.

I wanted to give my own little contribution as well.

Taking advantage of the wonderful online community of data lovers, I’ve compiled a curated list of free learning resources on various data-related topics. The resources include both video courses and books.

The quarantine gives us something that we usually dream of: more time. Time to reflect, to take stock of our lives and to catch up with things that we’ve put off for a while, including our personal learning goals.

Humans don’t respond too well to having an abundance of choices, so I tried to keep this resource list short. My choices of learning material naturally reflect my own inclinations, but I tried to cover a wide spectrum of topics.

Video courses

  • Intro to Deep Learning with Tensorflow - Udacity
  • All fast.ai deep learning courses
  • AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner Training - FreeCodeCamp
  • Intro to Hadoop and MapReduce - Udacity
  • All the courses on cognitiveclass.ai
  • Probability and Statistics in Data Science using Python - UC San Diego
  • Docker Containers Tutorial - FreeCodeCamp

Books

  • Interpretable Machine Learning - Christoph Molnar
  • Bayesian Methods for Hackers - Cam Davidson-Pilon
  • The Hundred-Page Machine Learning Book - Christoph Molnar
  • Think Bayes - Allen B. Downey
  • Mathematics for Machine Learning - Marc Peter Deisenroth, A. Aldo Faisal, Cheng Soon Ong
  • Data Engineering Cookbook - Andreas Kretz
  • Kafka - The Definitive Guide - Neha Narkhede, Gwen Shapira & Todd Palino
  • Cloud Data Management - The Data School

Github Awesome Lists

If you’re not familiar with GitHub Awesome, it’s a collectively-sourced project that gathers in a single place various interesting (or should I say, awesome) resources divided by topic.

Here you can find a directory of topics, though I’m not sure how complete it is. It can certainly give you enough material to last multiple weeks.

  • Awesome Data Engineering
  • Awesome Data Science
  • Awesome Production Machine Learning
  • Awesome Data Visualization
  • Awesome Public Datasets

That’s it from me. If you have some free resource you want to share, feel free to add it in the comments so everyone can benefit.

Stay safe - and stay home,

Carolina
